About Corey

Good day, I welcome you to a Portfolio by Corey. Here you will find blood, sweat and code. Where it all started: well, having spread my wings and fled the comfort of my bedroom I found myself digging deep into music technology, more specifically music production where I've spent many years ruining my ears! Through self teaching, trial and error I've reached a level where I can recreate a song within a DAW just by listening a few times around, awesome right! Fast forward a few years and i'm ready to take my skills further, being invested heart and head into crypto currency and fascinated with blockchain technologies I find myself partaking in a full stack web development boot-camp, the rest is history.
